Managing Medicines Shouldn’t Mean Managing Waste

As pharmacists, we see it every day—valuable medicines going unused, expiring on the shelves, or being wasted because they weren’t tracked on time. In Pune and across Maharashtra, studies show that over 70% of medicines from households end up discarded in the trash due to lack of proper awareness and monitoring.

For a medical store owner, this isn’t just a public health issue—it directly affects business. Expired stock means lost revenue, shrinking profit margins, and sometimes even penalties if records aren’t maintained properly.

Why does this happen?

  • Inventory isn’t always updated in real time
  • Medicines expire silently on the shelves without alerts
  • Manual record-keeping makes it easy to miss dates
  • Lack of a central system for reminders or stock movement

MEDI-TRACK is built to solve this problem for pharmacists. From smart stock management to expiry alerts, our platform helps medical stores reduce wastage, cut losses, and ensure medicines reach patients while they’re still effective.
